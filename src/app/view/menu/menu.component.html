► How to play

Kick bombs to the opponent's field! When the time runs out, the team with the most tiles left wins.
Tip: stop near holes to start repairing.
Player 1: WASD + Q
Player 2: Arrow keys + Enter
Player 3: IJKL + Space
Player 4: Numpad 8456 + Numpad+
